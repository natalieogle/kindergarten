About

Hi, I'm Natalie and I love to code! If I'm not at my current day job as an analyst, riding my horse, or snuggling my kitties, you will find me glued to my computer.

I've always enjoyed making things and being creative. I also find immense satisfaction in solving problems. I think programming is a perfect mix of these things.

Over the last ten months, I have been spending every moment of my free time learning JavaScript and Ruby on Rails and working on projects. I used to think I was borderline narcoleptic, but my problem wasn't physical. It was mental. I can officially say I have caught the programmer bug and now have trouble turning off my brain when it is bedtime!

My career path up to this point has been anything but linear, but I can't express how excited I am to have finally found what I want to do when I grow up!
