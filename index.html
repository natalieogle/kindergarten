About

Hi, I'm Natalie and I love to program! If I'm not at my current day job as a data analyst, riding my horse, or snuggling my kitties, you will find me glued to my computer.

I've always enjoyed making things, being creative, and solving problems. I think programming is a perfect mix of these things.

Over the last year, I have been spending every moment of my free time learning JavaScript and Ruby on Rails and working on projects. More recently I have been working on some issues in GitHub's text editor, Atom. One of my changes (most recently used tab switching) will even be released in version 1.7.0 of Atom!

My career path up to this point has been anything but linear, but I'm super excited I finally figured out what I want to do when I grow up!
