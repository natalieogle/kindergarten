Jot is a full-stack JavaScript scheduling application I am building with a partner that will be used by a Portland private school for their childcare services. It will allow both the school and caregivers to easily coordinate and manage before and after-school care.

Technology stack: ExpressJS, Orchestrate, BackboneJS, PassportJS, HandlebarsJS, JQuery, HTML, Bootstrap, and CSS

Next stages: Adding admin interface, additional user features, push notifications, and payment gateway
